# PMR Interview Guide Template

## Goal
State the learning goal for this interview round.

## Interview target
- Segment:
- Role:
- Why this person matters:

## Opening
- Brief context
- Make it clear you are learning, not pitching

## Core questions
1. Walk me through the last time you faced this problem.
2. What happened first?
3. How often does this happen?
4. What is the cost or consequence when it happens?
5. How do you handle it today?
6. What is frustrating about the current approach?
7. Who else is involved in the decision or workflow?
8. What have you tried before?
9. What would make this worth paying for?
10. What would stop you from changing?

## Evidence to capture
- exact language used
- current workaround
- cost / delay / risk
- urgency signal
- willingness-to-pay signal
- contradictions

## End note
- Ask for intros if appropriate
- Ask what other people like them would say
