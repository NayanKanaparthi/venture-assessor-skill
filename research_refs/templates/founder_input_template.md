# Founder Input Template

Use this when the founder's starting input is too vague.

## Idea
- What is the idea or concept in one sentence?

## Problem hypothesis
- What problem do you think exists?

## Target user
- Who do you think has this problem?

## Buyer
- Who do you think would pay?

## Current workaround
- How do people solve this today?

## Why now
- Why does this matter now?

## Geography / industry scope
- Any market boundaries to assume?

## Constraints
- Team strengths, timing, capital, access, or other constraints?

## Existing evidence
- Any interviews, pilots, signups, paid tests, or market signals already available?
